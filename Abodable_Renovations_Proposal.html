The Process

How It Works

A streamlined, four-step journey from imagination to installation.

1

Share Your Space

Send us photos of your current kitchen. Our team reviews your existing layout, cabinet structure, and overall space to understand your starting point.

2

Design Consultation

We discuss your preferred styles, materials, colors, and hardware preferences. Our specialists guide you through options that suit your taste and goals.

3

Virtual Visualization

Mile High AI Labs renders photorealistic images of your new kitchen. Review and refine your design until it's exactly what you envisioned — before spending a dollar on materials.

4

Proposal & Build

You receive a detailed price proposal aligned with your approved design. Once confirmed, our team handles the full renovation from start to stunning finish.
